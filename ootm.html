Odyssey of the Mind

Odyssey of the Mind is a problem-solving organization that fosters creativity. I participated in Odyssey of the Mind for two years, and I really enjoyed the experience. The topic that my team chose was designing and building a balsa wood structure, which had to weigh 15 grams or less. The goal of the project was for our structure to withstand as much weight as possible without breaking. After countless hours of designing and testing, we created a final structure that withstood over 600 lbs in weight. Our team came in 2nd place in the Regional Competition and advanced to the State Competition.
